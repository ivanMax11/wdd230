Forum international convention center

The Santiago del Estero Forum, Convention and Exhibition Center is a building located in the city of Santiago del Estero (Argentina) that is intended for meetings, congresses, seminars and conventions among other uses. It is located in one of the historic buildings of the city that was in disuse: the old Miter railway station, for which it was recovered and put into value.
